What is Break2Future

CULTURE.
BATTLE.
COMMUNITY.

Break2Future is Croatia's premier breaking event — a two-day celebration of breaking culture that brings together some of the most talented B-Boys and B-Girls from across the region and beyond.

From beginner-level kids battles to elite international showdowns, B2F is a platform for every level of dancer. Featuring workshops led by world-class artists, panel discussions, and competitive battles across multiple formats.

Held in the heart of Zagreb across two iconic venues, Break2Future is where the next generation of breakers get their moment.
